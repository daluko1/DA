What is Bullying?

Bullying can happen anywhere, in schools, at home,... It can take many forms, including physical, verbal, and cyberbullying.

Forms of Bullying

  • Physical bullying
  • Verbal bullying
  • Cyberbullying

Talk to someone

There are many resources available to help victims of bullying. Some of these include:

  • Hotlines
  • Counseling services
  • Support groups

How to Prevent Bulling

There are many ways that you can get involved in the fight against bullying. Some of these include:

  • Volunteering
  • Donating
  • Raising awareness
